# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 2026_

HangryHippo is a free, open-source personal nutrition tracker. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

---

## What we collect

Your email address used to log in, and the nutrition data you enter: meal plans, recipes, foods, and notes. Nothing else. We do not collect analytics, usage telemetry, device identifiers, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

## How it is used

Your data is used for one purpose only: providing HangryHippo to you.

Your data is **never sold** to any third party. Your data is **never shared** with any third party. Your data is **never used** for advertising, profiling, research, or any purpose other than running your own account.

## Where it is stored

Your data is stored in a secure, encrypted cloud database. Each account is fully isolated and no account can access another account's data.

## Your rights

**Export** - download a full copy of your data at any time from the Settings page.

**Deletion** - to delete your account and all associated data, use the option in the settings, which irreversibly deletes all your data or contact us via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/denny190/hangryhippo/issues).

---

Questions or concerns? Open an issue at [github.com/denny190/hangryhippo](https://github.com/denny190/hangryhippo/issues).
